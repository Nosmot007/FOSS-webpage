ABOUT FOSS

A FOSS Club, or Free and Open-Source Software Club, is an organization or community group that focuses on promoting, supporting, and advocating for the use of free and open-source software (FOSS). Free and open-source software refers to software that is both free to use and open for anyone to inspect, modify, and distribute its source code. FOSS clubs play a crucial role in fostering a culture of collaboration, sharing, and transparency in the realm of software development and technology.

At the heart of our FOSS club lies a deep passion for collaboration, innovation, and the belief that technology should be accessible to all
